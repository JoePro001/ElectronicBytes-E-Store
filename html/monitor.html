I never expected this

Reviewed in the United States on April 27, 2023

It comes with a fully adjustable base, a big plus in my case. It is vesa mountable. I am running this 32 inch monitor with an MSI 1070 GPU and it's running it just fine, rarely dropping below frame rates into the 60's it pretty much stays around 100. I thought I was going to have to upgrade my GPU and Motherboard to run this, but decided to hook it up anyway and its working just fine. Doom Eternal runs fine with everything set on high. I never expected that. This model has historically had very volatile pricing.

I considered buying the 165hz instead, and had to dig a lot to find out why that pricing is so wonky. I think because the 165hz model is a 1000 R curve and this 144hz model being reviewed is a 1500 R curve. Like many I assume, the 1000 R curve would be a just little to aggressive a curve to fit on my desk top.

The first monitor had a fork lift hole right through the front of the monitor. Amazon covered it up with tape and shipped it that way, but made it right in the end and shipped me another. It has been great for over 6 months now, It has played everything I throw at it. It comes with a three year no dead pixel warranty. There are no LED's on the back of either model. The red accents are just stickers that do not light up.
