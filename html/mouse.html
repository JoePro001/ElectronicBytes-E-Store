great little mouse

Reviewed in the United States on July 18, 2023

This is a petite mouse, perfect for small hands or travel. It is very comfortable to use and responsive for daily work. As a matter of fact I'm using it right now, well not exactly right now because I'm typing.

Anyway, I read a couple of reviews that the USB receiver was missing. I'm not sure that they were looking for it in the right place. The USB receiver is conveniently stored in the battery compartment. This is awesome! Especially when traveling, because it won't fall off and get lost. Lost USB is spoken from experience as that is exactly what happened to the mouse I am replacing. I lost the USB somewhere between home and a visit out of state, yes, somewhere that little USB probably got sucked up into a vacuum cleaner and I was left with a useless hunk of plastic.
