Not really USB C

Reviewed in the United States on May 16, 2023

The charging socket is shaped like USB C but, with all of my standard USB C chargers, it accepts power with only one orientation, not both. So, you have to plug it in, confirm that it is charging and, if it is not, turn the charging cable over. The supplied charger works in either orientation, so they understand what they have not done properly in the phone.

I am buying nothing except standard USB C devies and have chargers in convenient locations around the house plus a powerpack. This phone has the problem with all of them. I refuse to use devices that don't properly support my existing chargers.
